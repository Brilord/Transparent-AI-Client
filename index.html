Plana help

Capture

Add a link with URL, optional title, tags, folder, priority, and notes. Use drag-and-drop or the clipboard banner to capture faster.

Links list

Click a card to open its window. Use inline actions for open in browser, copy, edit, pin, favorite, or delete.

Search and tags

Search filters by title, URL, tags, and notes. Toggle fuzzy/exact. Tag chips filter the list; right-click a chip to pin it.

Quick access

Recents shows recently opened links. Frequently used ranks links by open count.

Workspaces

Save workspace captures currently open link windows and their positions. Open restores them; delete removes the workspace.

Command palette

Press Ctrl/Cmd+K to open the palette. Type to search, Enter to open, use action buttons, Esc to close.

Options

Use the gear for transparency, always-on-top, resizer injection, persistence, launch on startup, folder sync, storage file, background, reset, and telemetry.

Window controls and shortcuts

Drag the top bar to move. Resize from edges (if resizers are enabled). Shortcuts: Alt+Left/Right (Windows/Linux) and Cmd+[ / Cmd+] (macOS) for back/forward in link windows, mouse back/forward buttons, Ctrl+Alt+Arrows resize, Ctrl+Alt+Shift+Arrows move, Ctrl+Alt+M maximize, Ctrl+Alt+C center, Ctrl+Alt+R reset window.
